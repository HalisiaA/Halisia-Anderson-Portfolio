The Purpose of Each Page

  • Index Page:

    • The homepage of my portfolio website. It introduces visitors to my website and provides an overview of who I am, my interests, and what they can expect to find on my website.

  • Professional Page:

    • This page contains detailed information about my professional background, including my resume, work experience, education history, skills, professional vision statement, mission statement, and job requirements for my desired career path.

  • Personality Page:

    • This page highlights my personality traits, emotions, hobbies, and how they impact my academic or school life. It also includes a favorite quote from someone I admire, as well as an assessment of my personality using an online personality tool.

  • Personal Development Page:

    • This page summarizes and reflects on the content from Chapters 4-7 of "Twelve Pillars" by Jim Rohn and Chris Widener, discussing how I could apply the principles discussed in my own life. It also includes the name of the degree I am working towards and the top three skills I have acquired so far.

  • Storyboard Page:

    • This page serves as a storyboard or wireframe for my website, providing a visual layout design, a list of my pages and their purpose, a theme of my site, and a list of features I intend to use on the website.

  • Reference Page:

    • This page includes all the bibliographic sources of information for used assistance, such as books, websites, etc., in APA format. It provides references for any external sources used in my portfolio website.

    The Theme of Each Page

    I opted for blue, purple, and red as my site's main colors for their simple yet professional and clean aesthetic, reflecting my personality.
